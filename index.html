About

I'm Aira Jhean Paulite Rivero, a 19-year-old resident of Banquerohan, Legazpi City, and a dedicated 3rd-year BSIT student. Growing up with two younger brothers, I've learned the importance of perseverance and setting a positive example. Even though I occasionally find myself facing challenges and uncertainties in the IT field, my family's support and my determination keep me going.

My journey in IT has just begun, and I am on a quest to discover where my true interests and talents lie within this dynamic field. While my project portfolio may be in its early stages, I am committed to honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. It's the drive to provide a better future for my family and to inspire others, just as my younger brothers inspire me, that fuels my passion for IT.

I invite you to join me on this exciting journey of growth, exploration, and learning as I continue to pursue my dreams in the world of Information Technology.
